Many of these projects have end goals of serving Epson's industrial robots.

While I cannot elaborate on the know-how development and product uses of the projects I worked on, I can share some of my responsibilities and the impacts I created.

Contributions

Neural Radiance Fields (NerF)

I implemented a novel NeRF model that trains on a set of sparse input views and renders 3D scenes from unseen views. This technology assisted with downstream tasks for Epson's industrial robots.

3D Object Detection

I developed an algorithm to detect and label spherical objects on depth maps. This eliminated the requirement for manual effort when labelling depths data. The algorithm achieved greater accuracy compared to manual labelling. I also assisted in detecting objects with arbitrary shapes in an image.

Differentiable Rendering

I assisted in applying differentiable rendering to render photorealistic images. This technology was used in downstream tasks by the computer vision and robotics team.

Depth Prediction from Images

I trained machine learning models to predict depth information of a robot's workspace, evaluation results are within three millimeters of error.
